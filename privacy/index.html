SimpleType.

Privacy

SimpleType doesn't receive anything you type, but your browser's configuration and other factors could lead to a third party receiving the things you type.

Browser

Browser extensions

Make sure that the browser extensions that you are using are trustworthy. Some extensions such as Grammarly and LanguageTool receive everything you type. Make sure to check their privacy policy or disable the extension when privacy is needed.

Smartphone keyboard

Some digital keyboards might collect the things you type. Check your keyboard's privacy policy.
