SimpleType.

About

SimpleType is a temporary typing web application that respects your privacy. You can use it to communicate, to use typing assistants like LanguageTool and Grammarly, or for any other reason you may want.

How to use and tips

  1. Go to SimpleType
  2. Type what you want
  3. Done!

You can remove the navigation button “⋮” on handheld devices by holding them in landscape. Making the text you wrote easier to read.

Privacy Main page SimpleType doesn't receive anything you type, though your browser configuration could result in a third party receiving everything you type.
